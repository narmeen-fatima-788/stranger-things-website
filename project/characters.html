MEET THE GANG

Meet the brave kids of Hawkins who face the Upside Down. When strange things began to happen in Hawkins, these kids did not run away. They stood together, ready to face the unknown and protect what they loved.

ELEVEN

Once a mysterious girl with extraordinary powers, Eleven continues to grow into her strength, using her abilities to protect her friends and face the unanswered horrors of the Upside Down.

MIKE WHEELER

Loyal, brave, and always thinking of others, Mike stands at the heart of the group, holding everyone together no matter how strange things get.

WILL BYERS

Will 's brave spirit and deep connection to the Upside Down help him face future mysteries, proving that empathy and resilience matter most.

DUSTIN HENDERSON

Dustin's clever ideas and endless optimism keep the gang laughing, even when facing the strangest dangers of Hawkins.

LUCAS SINCLAIR

Bold, practical, and ready for action, Lucas uses his smarts and courage to help his friends stand strong against anything that comes their way.

MAX MAYFIELD

Fearless and determined, Max stays true to herself while standing alongside her friends, proving that bravery comes from the heart.

ERICA SINCLAIR

Iconic, Sharp, witty, and fearless, Erica steals every scene with her humor and cleverness, proving that size doesn't matter when it comes to bravery.

STEVE HARRINGTON

Everyone 's favorite “babysitter with a bat,” Steve's mix of charm, bravery, and heart makes him the hero Hawkins never knew it needed.

NANCY WHEELER

Smart, determined, and fearless, Nancy uses her wit and courage to chase the truth and stand strong against the dark secrets haunting Hawkins.

JONATHAN BYERS

Thoughtful and loyal, Jonathan faces danger with quiet strength, always looking out for his friends and fighting to uncover the mysteries lurking in the shadows.

ROBIN BUCKLEY

Sharp-witted, resourceful, and quick with a joke, Robin blends intelligence and bravery as she helps decode the Upside Down's most puzzling threats.

HENRY CREEL [VECNA]

Once a hidden force behind Hawkins' darkness, Henry known as Vecna remains a chilling mastermind who tests the limits of fear and mystery in the Upside Down.
